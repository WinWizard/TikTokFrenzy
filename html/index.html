How it works

  1. 1. Connect a Wallet

    Connect a Crypto wallet to interact with the TikTok Frenzy smart contract on the blockchain.

    The smartcontract is opensource and runs on the Binance Smart Chain.

  2. 2. Join a Team

    Enter the username of your Favorite Influencer and choose one yourself. By joining the TikTok Frenzy, you automatically become part of that influencer team.

  3. 3. Climb the leaderboard

    For every entry, you and your favorite influencer receive 100 TikTok Frenzy Points. Support your team or compete with other influencers by sharing your own username.

  4. 4. Win!

    When all the TikTok Frenzy Points are distributed the score become final, the users who joined the team of the winning influencers veryone in the team can withdraw their profit.

Exclusive decentralised points system
